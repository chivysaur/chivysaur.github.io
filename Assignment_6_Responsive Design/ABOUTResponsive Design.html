Sweets Glore!

We first opened our doors in 2021 around the borders of Chinatown and the Financial District of Lower Manhattan. Our style mixes Asian and French flavors together to recreate many classic sweets that we all love!

Our pastries are made with the finest ingredients and baked fresh daily. Our coffee is roasted in Brooklyn, packaged while the beans are still warm, and shipped to our stores within 24 hours of roasting for maximum flavor and freshness.
